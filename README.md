## 🧠 What is GigGuardian AI?

GigGuardian AI is an **intelligent safety + income protection platform** designed for gig delivery riders working with platforms like Blinkit and Zepto.

Every day, riders work in:
- 🌧 Heavy rain  
- 🌡 Extreme heat (>45°C)  
- 🌫 Hazardous air (AQI > 300)  
- 🌪 Cyclones & floods  

👉 With **no warning system**  
👉 And **zero financial protection**

---

## 🚨 The Problem

Delivery riders are the backbone of quick commerce — yet completely unprotected.

### ⚠ Safety Risks
- Road accidents during heavy rain  
- Heatstroke in extreme temperatures  
- Breathing issues due to pollution  
- Flood-prone routes with no guidance  
- No alerts during disasters  

### 💸 Financial Risks
- 20–30% weekly income loss per disruption  
- No insurance or compensation  
- No visibility into upcoming risks  
- Families impacted instantly  

### ❗ The Core Issue
> Riders must choose:  
> **Risk their life OR lose their income**

## 💡 Our Solution

**GigGuardian AI = Smart Bodyguard + Income Insurance**

✔ Predicts dangerous conditions

✔ Automatically compensates income loss  

> No forms. No claims. No waiting. Fully automated.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works (5-Step Pipeline)

1. **Data Ingestion**  
   Weather, AQI, disaster & traffic data  

2. **AI Risk Scoring**  
   Random Forest model generates zone risk score (0–1)  

3. **Early Alert System**  
   Riders get alerts **30+ minutes before danger**  

4. **Rider Decision**
   - Log off safely  
   - Continue (Hazard Mode)  

5. **Auto Compensation**
   - Triggered automatically  
   - Payout within **< 2 hours**
